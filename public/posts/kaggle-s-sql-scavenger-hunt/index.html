March 8, 2018

There were 5 different tasks sent out over 5 days to answer some questions making use of SQL and BigQuery.

Overall, I thought that the assignments were well written and helped guide me through the questions. However, I still had some room to experiment and see how the packages worked. Thanks Rachel for putting these together!
